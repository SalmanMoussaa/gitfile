computer Science

what is computer Science:

Computer science is the study of computing devices and methods. Computer scientists work primarily with software and software systems, including their theory, design, development, and application, in contrast to electrical and computer engineers.

history:

In the 1950s and early 1960s, computer science started to become recognized as a separate academic field. The University of Cambridge Computer Laboratory launched the first computer science degree program in the world, the Cambridge Diploma in Computer Science, in 1953.
